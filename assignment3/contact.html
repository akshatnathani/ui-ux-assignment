Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Is there any registration fee?

A: No, TechFest 2025 is completely free for all participants. There are no registration or participation fees.

Q: Can I participate in multiple events?

A: Yes! You can register for multiple events. However, please check the schedule to ensure there are no timing conflicts between your chosen events.

Q: Are team events allowed?

A: Yes, many events like robotics and ML hackathon allow teams of 2-5 members. Individual events are clearly marked in the events list.

Q: What should I bring on event days?

A: Please bring your college ID card, registration confirmation, and any specific equipment mentioned for your events. Laptops are required for programming and web development events.

Q: Is accommodation available?

A: Yes, we can arrange accommodation for outstation participants. Please mention this requirement in your registration form or contact us at least one week before the event.

Q: Will food be provided?

A: Yes, lunch and refreshments will be provided to all registered participants on all three days. Please mention any dietary restrictions in your registration form.

Q: What are the prizes?

A: Prize money ranges from ₹10,000 to ₹35,000 depending on the event. Winners also receive certificates and trophies. All participants get participation certificates.

Q: Can I change my registered events?

A: Yes, you can modify your event selection up to 3 days before the event starts. Contact the coordinators via email to make changes.

Q: Is there a dress code?

A: Casual formal attire is recommended. Please wear your college ID cards throughout the event for identification purposes.

Q: How do I get to the venue?

A: The college is well-connected by public transport. Detailed directions and campus maps will be sent via email after registration. Free parking is available on campus.
